About

I am an instructor and booking specialist with a focus on analytics and education using digital programs. I aspire to visually contribute to the growing and vast field of Information Systems and explore digital solutions for different businesses and organizations. I strive to integrate my research skills from academic and transdisciplinary real-world experience to provide insightful decisions through visual storytelling with data.

I demonstrate strong problem-solving, analytical thinking, curiosity, breaking complex ideas and concepts, and communication skills. I aim to offer my expertise to solve meaningful problems in environmental sustainability by bridging Environmental, Engineering, Technology, Business, Communications, and Operations fields. My technical skills include data analysis, data visualization, Adobe, GIS, Python, R, and SQL.
